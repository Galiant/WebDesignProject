DRUMBEAT

A unique design feature of the yacht is the layout on one level from cockpit through the saloon and dining area to the forward wheelhouse. This creates an expansive area for entertaining.

Drumbeat has just undergone a major interior refit in 2005. The interior has been redesigned to reflect the simplicity and beauty of the lines of this stunning sailing yacht. The colours, materials and furniture are designed for comfort. Lighting has been brought to the highest standard giving the option of setting the appropriate mood to the saloon and throughout the yacht. The overall feeling is one of casual elegance.
