TITANIA

Titania's guests are hosted in 7 fantastic cabins, including two Master Suites, one on the main deck with a private Jacuzzi and the other is an impressive newly installed second Master Suite on the upper deck with a panoramic sea view. She also has a VIP Cabin on the bridge deck plus four double cabins on the lower deck. There is an elevator going from the lower deck to the sun deck.

She has a fully equipped gym on the sun deck with fitness instructor and a beach club with sauna which can be converted into a night club. There is also a spa with massage facilities and a huge array of water toys with a very service orientated, charter friendly crew.

Her vast deck spaces can host a dockside party for up to 200 guests, with the beach club converting to an exclusive floating nightclub.

Titania carries two tenders of which one is limo, two kayaks, four jetskis, two sailing dinghies, 10 bicycles, and scuba equipment. All guests cabins come with Creston Control A/V systems, satellite television, and extensive music and movie libraries.
